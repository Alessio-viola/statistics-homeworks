Properties of the Poisson Process

The Poisson Process exhibits several key properties:

  • Stationarity: The process has a constant average rate of events over time.
  • Memorylessness: The time until the next event is independent of the past.
  • Independent Increments: The number of events in disjoint time intervals is independent.
